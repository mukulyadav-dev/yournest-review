The Reset

We Reset to a Pull-Based System

We stopped all performance marketing
We didn't want to grow without managing efficiency — scaling up inefficiencies makes it impossible to create a pull-based system
The way to look at numbers changed — slope is more important than height
Still getting 100+ bookings every day. Zero push spend.
Fix 1

Some Customers Were Confused. We Fixed It.

Initially there were clarity-related issues in onboarding. Customers weren't sure how pay-as-you-go worked, what they'd pay, or when.

We redesigned the flow, simplified the messaging, and made the journey crystal clear.

Fix 2

Partners Needed Clarity Too. We Fixed Incentives.

Install efficiency for online leads used to be ~48%, then dropped to ~40% with confusion and no ground support.

We streamlined incentives and made the process clearer for partners.

~48% → ~40% ↓ initial drop
Assign-to-install ratio (post fix)
55% → 77% ↑ 40% jump
